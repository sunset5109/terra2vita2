What We Do

Our Programs

Three programs built to address water and climate challenges through technology, education, and research.

Technology · Active

WaterWise

An AI-powered irrigation recommendation app that combines physics-informed neural networks with real-time satellite data. Deployed across 50 global sites with validated 22.3% water savings and support for 32+ million people in 10+ languages.

Explore WaterWise →
Education · Active

Little Builders

Free, hands-on climate education workshops for elementary students built around inquiry-based learning. Our 2025 summer sessions run July 12 and July 19 at Northeast Regional Library. 35+ students served, 100% satisfaction rate.

Rise2Research

A student research showcase platform connecting young scientists with mentors, audiences, and opportunities. Rise2Research will provide a structured space for student-led research to be seen, supported, and celebrated.
