Featured Opportunity

Become a Founding Ambassador

Terra2Vita's Founding Ambassador program is for students, educators, and professionals who want to actively represent and grow our mission in their communities. As a Founding Ambassador, you'll be among the first cohort shaping what this program becomes.

Founding Ambassadors help spread awareness of WaterWise, support Little Builders workshops, and advocate for water equity and climate education in their schools and communities.

What you get as a Founding Ambassador

  • Official recognition as a Terra2Vita Founding Ambassador
  • Access to our network of researchers and professionals
  • Opportunity to help shape the program's direction
  • Certificate of participation and letter of support
